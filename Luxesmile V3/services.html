Ask a Question
Does teeth whitening work on crowns and veneers?
No — whitening agents only work on natural tooth enamel. Crowns, veneers, and bonding will not change colour. If you have restorations, we'll advise whether whitening the natural teeth first (before new restorations) is a better approach.
How long does a dental implant procedure take?
The implant placement surgery takes about 1–2 hours per implant. However, the total process (from placement to final crown) typically takes 3–6 months to allow the implant to fully fuse with the jawbone (osseointegration). We provide a temporary tooth during this period.
Is orthodontic treatment suitable for adults?
Absolutely! There's no age limit for orthodontic treatment. In fact, we see a large number of adult patients. Options like Invisalign and ceramic braces are especially popular with adults because they're discreet and don't affect your professional appearance.
Does Luxesmile accept NHIF or private insurance?
Yes! We accept SHA (formerly NHIF), Jubilee Health Insurance, AAR, Resolution Health, Madison, and most other major insurance providers in Kenya. We also accept NHIF and most corporate schemes. Please bring your insurance card and we'll verify coverage before treatment.
What are your payment options?
We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, mobile banking, cash, and all major insurance plans. For high-value treatments like implants or orthodontics, we offer affordable monthly instalment plans. Ask our patient coordinator for details.
