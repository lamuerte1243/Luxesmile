Why Work With Us

Current Job Openings at Luxesmile

All roles are based at our Ruiru and Ruai clinics in Nairobi. We welcome applications from across Kenya and the East Africa region.

Senior Dentist / Associate Dentist

We're looking for an experienced BDS-qualified dentist to join our busy general dentistry department. Implant experience is an advantage. Mentorship of junior team members expected.

Full-Time Clinical Nairobi

Orthodontist (Specialist)

Certified orthodontist required to lead our growing orthodontic department. Invisalign certification preferred. Excellent patient communication skills essential.

Full-Time Specialist Nairobi

Dental Hygienist

Qualified dental hygienist to handle preventive care, scaling, polishing, and patient oral health education. At least 2 years clinical experience required.

Full-Time Clinical Nairobi

Dental Laboratory Technician

Experienced lab tech to work in our on-site laboratory crafting crowns, veneers, dentures, and orthodontic appliances. CAD/CAM experience is a strong advantage.

Full-Time Laboratory Nairobi

Patient Coordinator / Receptionist

The face of Luxesmile! You'll manage appointments, patient queries, billing, and insurance claims. Excellent communication skills and experience in a medical or hospitality setting essential.

Full-Time Administration Nairobi
